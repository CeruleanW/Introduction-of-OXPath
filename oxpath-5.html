Why do we use OXPath

  • Simulate interactions with complicated pages: enter words, move your mouse, click, etc.
  • Scale well in performance with relatively low time and memory use
  • Page iteration: Go through linked multiple pages as you wish
  • Select things by their color, size, or other presentation style via CSS selecting methods
  • Good maintenance for durable web data extraction
